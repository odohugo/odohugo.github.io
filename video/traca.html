o voo da traça morta

video, 247'46 (2020)

A moth makes its last flight, looking for a place to die.
This allegorical trip was represented in a audiovisual piece with the duration of the gallery's daily opening hours.
Every day, the piece played once and only fragments of it were seen, never its totality.
The video begins with a lot of distortion and noise which slowly softens along its 4 hour duration, transforming into different landscapes and melodies, ending with the final place chosen by the moth.
