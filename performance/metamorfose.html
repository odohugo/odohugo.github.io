metamorfose + uns minutos com o meu avô

live performance with video (2019)

Sitting at a table, an eraser was used from beginning to end, worn down to small pieces on a piece of cardboard.
While this action occurred, the video Uns minutos com o meu avô was shown on one of the room's walls.
In that video, I am sitting at a table, listening to a song that reminds me of my late grandfather and thinking of moments we spent together.
The music repeatedly alternates between playing at normal volume and playing almost no sound, making it is possible to hear children playing in the background.
The performance ended when the eraser was fully worn out.
